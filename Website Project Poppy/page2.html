Get Reusing

Instead of buying a new plastic or can deodorant every month that you throw away, why not try out a no waste brand like Fussy or Wild? Both brands offer subscriptions or one-off buys and have really great scents! Our favourite pick is Wild's Sandlewood and Patchouli scent. Think about switching other plastics such as bottles to glass and toothbrushes to bamboo.

Buy Vintage

By buying clothes second hand, we can reduce the need for the production of garments. Great stores like Thredup, Depop, The Clear Out Store, Vinted and Thrift+ offer really great deals. You could try selling your unwanted items too! Shops like Thrift+ offer reselling platforms for you to sell unwanted clothes for money or redeemable points.

Shop Local

If we can all shop locally we can reduce the carbon produced during the transit of goods and help local economy. Try shopping at a local market for fresh produce. If you're up for the change, going vegan or vegetarian is really beneficial for the planet. Brands like Beyond Meat, Green Cuisine and Sheese offer great no-meat products.
